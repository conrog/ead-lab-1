Pine Copse Studio

Pine Copse Studio is a professional recording studio located in South Dublin, featuring the best of both analogue and modern digital tools.

Our Services

Audio Visual Recording

Pine Copse Studio contains all the equipment you would need. You can bring your own or use guitars, drums, pianos, microphones and amps which are already onsite.

Mixing And Remastering

We can adjust and combine any individual tracks into your desired format. The studio contains large monitors which provide the best possible audio playback for us to achieve the mix that you desire.

Audio Sound Designing

At Pine Copse Studio we can cater to your postproduction needs. We have a wide range of audio interfaces, and we are experienced in using the latest software.
