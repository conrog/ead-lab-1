Pine Copse Studios

Pine Copse Studio is a project studio located in Dundrum, South County Dublin.

Our Services

Audio Visual Recording

Pine Copse Studio comes contains all the equipment you would need. You can bring your own or use guitars, drums, pianos, microphones and amps which are already onsite.

Mixing And Remastering

We can adjust and combine any individual tracks into your desired format. The studio contains large monitors which provide the best possible audio playback for us to achieve the mix that you desire.

Audio Sound Designing

At Pine Copse Studio we can cater to your post production needs. We have a wide rang of audio interfaces and are experience with the latest software.
